Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Short version: Continuum collects no data about you. Everything stays on your device and your own server. No analytics, no tracking, no third-party services.

Who we are

Continuum is an iOS app developed by Karl Wallbom. Open an issue at github.com/kalleeh/continuum/issues.

What data we collect

We collect nothing. Continuum does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data with us or any third party.

What happens on your device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

Data Where stored Purpose
Auth token iOS Keychain (device-only, no iCloud sync) Authenticates WebSocket connections to your server
WireGuard private key NetworkExtension Keychain (isolated from main app) Establishes encrypted VPN tunnel to your server
Server address & name UserDefaults (on-device) Displays your server in the UI
Session names UserDefaults (on-device) Displays session list
GitHub PAT (optional) iOS Keychain (device-only, no iCloud sync) Syncs repositories to your server on request
Terminal preferences UserDefaults (on-device) Font size, colour scheme, keyboard layout

None of this data is backed up to iCloud, shared with us, or transmitted anywhere except to your own server over the encrypted WireGuard tunnel.

Network connections

Continuum makes two types of outbound connections:

  • WireGuard VPN — UDP to your server's public IP on port 51820. This is the tunnel that carries all other traffic.
  • WebSocket connections — to 10.100.0.1 (your server's WireGuard address) on ports 7681 and 7682, inside the encrypted tunnel. These carry terminal I/O and Claude Code session data.

No connections are made to Anthropic, AWS, or any other third party. Your AI credentials (AWS Bedrock keys) are configured on your server, not in the app.

Push notifications

If you enable notifications, Apple Push Notification service (APNs) is used to deliver alerts when a Claude Code session finishes. Notification payloads contain only the session name and the text "Session finished" — no conversation content, no code, no personal data.

Your APNs device token is registered with your own server. It is not shared with us.

Camera

The camera is used only during onboarding to scan the WireGuard configuration QR code. The camera feed is processed on-device; no images are captured, stored, or transmitted.

Analytics and crash reporting

There are none. Continuum contains no analytics SDK, no crash reporter, and no telemetry of any kind.

Children's privacy

Continuum is a developer tool not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. Because we collect no data, changes are unlikely.
